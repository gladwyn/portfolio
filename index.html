More About Me

My interest in the IT field began back in secondary school when I joined the Infocomm Club as my CCA. While I was studying there, I came across the story of AlphaGo — the AI developed by Google , DeepMind that beat a world champion in the game of Go (Weiqi). I was amazed by what AI could do, and it deepened my curiosity about how these systems and algorithms work. That moment was a big reason I started considering a future in AI development

To build on this interest, I went on to take a course in Big Data and Analytics. It opened my eyes to the world of data science, and I found that I really enjoyed exploring data, applying machine learning techniques, and using visualizations to uncover insights and solve problems.

During my National Service, I worked as an Administrative Support Assistant (ASA), where I picked up a lot of soft skills — like working well in a team, dealing with people, and using tools like Microsoft Excel. I also got my first hands-on experience with Generative AI (GenAI) and saw how it can help me developed VBA code(s) and improve how things get done in the workplace.

More recently, I completed the 42 Piscine on 15 August 2025 — a challenging and intensive coding program that really pushed me to think critically and solve problems creatively. Next, I’ll be taking the next step in my journey by starting a Specialist Diploma in AI Solutions this October. I’m excited to keep learning and growing in this field that I’m passionate about.
