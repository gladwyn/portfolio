More About Me

I first developed an interest in the IT field when I joined the Infocomm Club as my CCA in secondary school. This early exposure sparked my passion for technology, which led me to pursue a Higher Nitec in Application Development at ITE. While studying at ITE, I was particularly inspired by the achievement of AlphaGo—an AI developed by Google that defeated a world champion in the game of Go (Weiqi). This remarkable milestone deepened my interest in artificial intelligence and motivated me to explore a career in AI development.

To further my education in this area, I enrolled in a Big Data and Analytics course, which closely aligns with the field of AI. Through this course, I discovered a strong aptitude for data science. I learned how to explore and analyze data, apply machine learning techniques, and use data visualizations to identify issues and propose effective solutions.

During my National Service, I worked as an Administrative Support Assistant (ASA). This role helped me develop valuable interpersonal skills, including teamwork, customer service, and proficiency in Microsoft Excel. I also gained exposure to Generative AI (GenAI) and learned how it can be leveraged to streamline workflows and improve efficiency.
